Violence Against Women: A Pain That Must Stop

Thousands and thousands of reports and articles were and are written about the dark side of being a women in Afghanistan, but the cruelties against women still continues every single day. There is not a day that we do not hear about violence against women in Afghanistan, about suffers, pains, and miseries they have to tolerate every day. What kind of work should be done to make our country a better place for our mothers, sisters, wives, and daughters is a significantly important question to ask? There are many programs and workshops to aware the women about their rights, however the result is not significant. Then, the answers for how to stop violence against women in Afghanistan should be reconsidered. A new solution and an effective way to decrease or stop this tragedy can be empowering women in society; so you give them power to defend themselves from any possible threat. Empowering women in society not only give them a chance to defend each other, but also make them independent individual to build our country. Women leadership and empowerment, can be a way to decrease violence more effectively in the society and pave the way for more women to come forward and take part to help themselves and their families and then the society. To conclude, violence against women in Afghanistan or in any parts of the world is not a new subject. Moreover, the ways to fight with it is different and unique in different situations. A solution to combat violence against women in Afghanistan can be empowering women in the society and give them the chance to prove themselves, therefore, they can be an example for any single women in Afghanistan.

Article by Marwa Faizi

Business Administration student at American University of Afghanistan
